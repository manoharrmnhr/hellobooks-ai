# Business Tax Basics

## GST (Goods and Services Tax) - India
Unified indirect tax replacing VAT, Service Tax, and Excise Duty.

### GST Types
- CGST: Central GST — intra-state (Centre's share)
- SGST: State GST — intra-state (State's share)
- IGST: Integrated GST — inter-state or imports

### GST Rates
- 0%: Essential food, health services
- 5%: Packaged food, economy transport
- 12%: Processed food, business services
- 18%: Most services, electronics, B2B SaaS
- 28%: Luxury goods, automobiles, tobacco

### Input Tax Credit (ITC)
GST Payable = Output Tax (GST Collected) minus Input Tax Credit (GST Paid on purchases)

### Key GST Returns
- GSTR-1: Outward supplies, due 11th of following month
- GSTR-3B: Monthly summary, due 20th of following month
- GSTR-9: Annual return, due December 31

## Income Tax for Businesses (India)
- Domestic Company default rate: 30%
- New Manufacturing Company (Section 115BAB): 15%
- Companies opting Section 115BAA: 22%
- Partnership/LLP: 30%

## Advance Tax
Due when annual liability exceeds Rs 10,000:
- 15 June: 15%
- 15 September: 45%
- 15 December: 75%
- 15 March: 100%

## TDS (Tax Deducted at Source)
- Section 194C Contractor payments: 1-2%
- Section 194J Professional fees: 10%
- Section 194H Commission: 5%
- Section 194I Rent commercial: 10%
- Section 192 Salaries: As per slab

## Depreciation under Income Tax Act
Uses Written Down Value (WDV) method:
- Furniture: 10%
- Computers: 40%
- Plant and Machinery: 15%
- Buildings commercial: 10%

## Tax Planning Tips
1. Maximize allowable deductions under Section 37
2. Claim depreciation on all eligible assets
3. Maintain proper documentation — all expenses need invoices
4. File returns on time to avoid Section 271 penalties
