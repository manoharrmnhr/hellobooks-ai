# Financial Ratios and KPIs

## Liquidity Ratios (Short-term solvency)

### Current Ratio
Current Ratio = Current Assets / Current Liabilities
- Above 2: Strong liquidity
- 1 to 2: Healthy
- Below 1: Potential cash crunch

### Quick Ratio (Acid-Test)
Quick Ratio = (Cash + AR + Short-term Investments) / Current Liabilities
More conservative — excludes inventory.

### Cash Ratio
Cash Ratio = (Cash + Cash Equivalents) / Current Liabilities
Most stringent liquidity test.

## Profitability Ratios

### Gross Profit Margin
GPM = Gross Profit / Net Revenue x 100

### Net Profit Margin
NPM = Net Profit / Net Revenue x 100

### Return on Assets (ROA)
ROA = Net Income / Total Assets x 100

### Return on Equity (ROE)
ROE = Net Income / Shareholders Equity x 100

## Leverage / Solvency Ratios

### Debt-to-Equity (D/E)
D/E = Total Debt / Total Shareholders Equity
- Below 1: Conservative
- 1 to 2: Moderate
- Above 2: High risk

### Interest Coverage Ratio (ICR)
ICR = EBIT / Interest Expense
- Above 3: Comfortable
- Below 1.5: Danger zone

## Efficiency / Activity Ratios

### Inventory Turnover
Inventory Turnover = COGS / Average Inventory (higher = faster moving)

### Days Sales Outstanding (DSO)
DSO = (AR / Annual Revenue) x 365 (lower = faster collection)

### Asset Turnover
Asset Turnover = Net Revenue / Total Assets

## Valuation Ratios

### EV/EBITDA
Enterprise Value divided by EBITDA — popular for startup and M&A valuation.

### Price-to-Earnings (P/E)
Market Price per Share / Earnings per Share

## Industry Benchmarks
- SaaS/Tech: Gross Margin 60-80%, Net Margin 10-25%
- Retail: Gross Margin 25-40%, Net Margin 2-5%
- Manufacturing: Gross Margin 20-35%, Net Margin 5-10%

## Analysis Best Practices
- Compare ratios against industry peers and own historical trends
- Use DuPont Analysis to decompose ROE into margin, turnover, leverage
- Watch for window dressing near reporting dates
